Books I've Read

I think it's fair that I don't remember all the books I've read, but here are a few of my favorites.
The Unwanteds #1: The first book in a series of I think 7, and I am currently rereading it. The world that it partly takes place in is so different than ours, that it is really fun to read about. Also, the things people don't know at the start can be really funny. The characters are not too perfect, or, in some cases, too evil (though some of them are quite evil), but just a good mix.
